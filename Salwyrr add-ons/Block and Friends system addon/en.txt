This addOn was made by JJJapla and Guga_Fenix, and here we will explain the addon and the commands.

The addon has a system of friendships and a system to block people. You can choose whether you want notification, which people, remove people, reset the add-on, etc.
The Friends addon contains 10 slots for friendships and the Block people addon contains 50 slots to block.

Do "/ addon help" to see all commands and availability!
